Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I apply for an internship?

A: Browse the "Available Internships" section, click "Apply Now" on any internship card, and submit with your resume.


Q: Is resume upload mandatory?

A: Resume upload is optional. You can submit your application even without a resume.


Q: How can I track my application status?

A: Check the "My Applications" section and the Progress Tracker to see your current status.
